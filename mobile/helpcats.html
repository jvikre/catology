How You Can Help!

Organizations you can donate to/volunteer at:

  • Cat Welfare Ohio
  • Colony Cats - Donate
  • Colony Cats - Volunteer
  • Columbus Pet Rescue
  • Pets Without Parents
  • American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals

Other Ways to Help

  • Donate to shelters - food, litter, and/or gently-used pet items, such as: water and food bowls, toys, leashes, collars, brushes/grooming tools, pet beds),
  • Foster shelter pets - this helps to socialize the animal while freeing up space in the shelter so they may take in more homeless animals
  • Ask others to donate to shelters in lieu of birthday/wedding presents
  • When looking to expand your fur-family, ADOPT!! Petfinder is a great website to use for this, or just visit your local shelter!
